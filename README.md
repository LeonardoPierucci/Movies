# List of Movies Seen from 2017-2022
A list of movies that I saw with my uncles and other friends during the years 2017 - 2022. For each movie, I recorded its Country, director, main actor, genre and category (taken from IMDB), the years in which they were dropped at cinema and when watched by us as well, score (the average of our single scores) and Duration in minutes.

My uncle is a cinema enthusiast, and for this reason he keeps track on a piece of paper of all the movies that he sees with his friends; I decided then to copy all the data inside an excel table, to perform simple statistic (like determine which country has the highest number of movies seen, which is the mean score, correlations between the length of a movie and the score etc.) and practicing my data analysis and coding skills, especially with Python and R.

The "(english)" version contains the columns' name in english, while in the other they are written in Italian. However, in both dataset, i tried to keep the original name for every movie.

I'm planning to update the database with the 2023-2026 years.
